## Section 3 — The problem

**Section label (small caps):**
The problem

**Headline:**
You're losing more time than you realise

**Body paragraph:**
Most small businesses are running on a mix of spreadsheets, emails, and manual steps that made sense when the business was smaller. Now those same processes are quietly costing you 15 to 25 hours a week — in time your team spends moving information from one place to another instead of doing actual work.

The frustrating part: most of it is automatable. Your team just doesn't know how.

**Pain point cards (icon + title + description):**

1. **Document processing**
   Invoices, contracts, and intake forms that someone reads, extracts information from, and captures manually — every single time.

2. **Data re-entry**
   The same information typed into your accounting software, your CRM, and a spreadsheet. Three times. By a person.

3. **Manual reporting**
   A weekly or monthly report that takes half a day to pull together from different sources — then sent by hand.

4. **Follow-up chasing**
   Client reminders, quote follow-ups, and appointment confirmations sent manually by someone who has better things to do.

## Section 4 — How it works

**Section headline:**
How it works

**Step 1:**
Title: AI Audit
Body: We spend two to three hours mapping your current processes together. You walk our team through how your business actually operates — we identify where the time is being lost and what's automatable. You leave with a clear picture of your biggest opportunities and a prioritised roadmap to act on them.

**Step 2:**
Title: Build & deploy
Body: Our team designs, builds, tests, and deploys your automations. Before anything gets built, we agree on exactly what's being built, what it will do, and what it won't. Fixed scope. No surprises. We handle everything — you review the result.

**Step 3:**
Title: Ongoing support
Body: Once your automations are running, we stay on as your automation partner. Monitoring, optimising, fixing edge cases, and building the next thing as your business grows. Your systems keep improving without you having to think about it.

## Section 5 — What we automate

**Section headline:**
What we automate

**Service cards (icon + title + description):**

1. **Workflow automation**
   Your apps and systems connected so information flows automatically — without anyone copying, pasting, or manually triggering the next step.

2. **Document processing**
   AI that reads your invoices, contracts, intake forms, and emails — extracts what matters, and routes it to the right place. No manual data capture.

3. **AI assistants**
   Custom AI tools trained on your business — your products, your processes, your knowledge. Your team gets answers instantly instead of asking the same questions over and over.

4. **Automated reporting**
   Reports that pull from your data sources, compile themselves, and land in your inbox on schedule. Monday morning ops reports. Weekly sales summaries. No manual work.

5. **Email & CRM workflows**
   Lead follow-ups, client reminders, quote sequences, and status updates sent automatically — at the right time, to the right person, without anyone hitting send.

6. **Customer service AI**
   An AI layer that handles your routine customer queries around the clock — FAQs, order status, appointment booking — and escalates only the ones that genuinely need a person.
